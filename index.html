Weiming Chen

Master Student in Xidian University

Xidian University

Weiming Chen received the B.Eng. degree in the mechanical design manufacture and automation from Xidian University, Xi’an, China, in 2019, where he is pursuing the M.Sc. degree in electronic science and technology with the Visual Information Processing Laboratory (VIPL), School of Electronic Engineering. His research interests include machine learning, computer vision, object detection, and remote sensing.
